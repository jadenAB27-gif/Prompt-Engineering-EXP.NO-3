EX.NO:3 Exploring the different types of prompts- Straightforward Prompts - Tabular Format Prompting - Missing Word Prompting - Preceding Question Prompting.

Aim: To write the prompts for the following prompt types for 2 different scenarios of Each type.

1.Straightforward Prompts

2.Tabular Format Prompting

3.Preceding Question Prompting

4.Missing Word Prompting

Explanation - Any one use case from Unit 5 or any 2 own use cases with the unit 2 Prompt type

Procedure:

Write 2 different prompts for different use cases, refer the following Examples

Example:

Straightforward Prompts:

• "Define photosynthesis in one sentence."

"List three advantages of electric vehicles."

Tabular Format Prompting:

• "Compare and contrast AC and DC current in a table."

• "Provide a table listing five programming languages, their paradigms, and one use case each."

Preceding Question Prompting:

• "Why is climate change a global concern? Explain how greenhouse gases contribute to global warming."

• "How do vaccines work? Describe the process of immunization in simple terms."

Missing Word Prompting:

• "The capital of France is ____."

• "In photosynthesis, plants absorb sunlight to produce ____."

OUTPUT:
## 1. Straightforward Prompts

### Scenario 1: Movies

**Prompt:**  
"List three characteristics of a superhero movie."

**Expected Output:**
- Hero protagonist
- Action sequences
- Conflict between hero and villain

---

### Scenario 2: Pop Culture

**Prompt:**  
"Define pop culture in one sentence."

**Expected Output:**  
"Pop culture refers to ideas, trends, entertainment, and activities that are popular within society at a given time."

---

## 2. Tabular Format Prompting

### Scenario 1: Movie Genres

**Prompt:**  
"Create a table comparing action, comedy, and horror movies based on theme, audience preference, and examples."

**Expected Output:**

| Genre | Theme | Audience Preference | Example |
|--------|--------|---------------------|----------|
| Action | Adventure and excitement | Thrill seekers | Avengers |
| Comedy | Humor and entertainment | Family audience | Home Alone |
| Horror | Fear and suspense | Horror fans | The Conjuring |

---

### Scenario 2: Popular Movie Franchises

**Prompt:**  
"Provide a table listing five popular movie franchises, their genre, and release decade."

**Expected Output:**

| Franchise | Genre | Release Decade |
|------------|--------|----------------|
| Star Wars | Science Fiction | 1970s |
| Harry Potter | Fantasy | 2000s |
| Fast & Furious | Action | 2000s |
| Jurassic Park | Adventure | 1990s |
| Marvel Cinematic Universe | Superhero | 2000s |

---

## 3. Preceding Question Prompting

### Scenario 1: Superhero Films

**Prompt:**  
"Why are superhero movies popular worldwide? Explain how storytelling and visual effects contribute to their success."

**Expected Output:**  
Superhero movies attract audiences because of engaging stories, memorable characters, action scenes, and advanced visual effects that create exciting cinematic experiences.

---

### Scenario 2: Social Media and Trends

**Prompt:**  
"How does social media influence pop culture? Describe how trends spread among audiences."

**Expected Output:**  
Social media spreads trends rapidly through sharing, videos, and online discussions, influencing fashion, music, language, and entertainment preferences.

---

## 4. Missing Word Prompting

### Scenario 1: Movies

**Prompt:**  
"Animated movies often attract ____ audiences."

**Expected Output:**  
`family`

---

### Scenario 2: Pop Culture

**Prompt:**  
"A movie released after the original film to continue the story is called a ____."

**Expected Output:**  
`sequel`

---
